Job Title: Machine Learning Engineer

We are looking for a Machine Learning Engineer with strong experience in Python,
TensorFlow or PyTorch, and deep learning. Responsibilities include building and
deploying ML models, working with pandas and numpy for data processing, and
collaborating with cross-functional teams. Experience with NLP, computer vision,
scikit-learn, and MLOps practices is a plus. Familiarity with Docker and cloud
platforms (AWS/GCP) is desirable. Strong understanding of neural networks, CNNs,
RNNs, and model evaluation metrics required.
